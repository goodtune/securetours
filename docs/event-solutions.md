# Event Solutions

<div class="st-meta" markdown>
Corporate · Sporting · Entertainment · Film & TV · Group
</div>

From intimate corporate functions to large-scale sporting spectacles and major film productions — AFS Group brings licensed security expertise, crowd management experience and white-glove hospitality to every event we support.

<div class="st-service-lead" markdown>
Secure Tours & Travel, as part of the Australian Frontline Solutions (AFS) Group, brings over 37 years of operational security and event management experience to every brief we accept. Our teams are not an add-on to your event — they are embedded within it.

Whether you are managing a delegation of corporate guests at a major sporting final, protecting a headline act on a national tour, coordinating the safe movement of hundreds of conference delegates across a city, or providing on-set security for a major film or television production, our licensed personnel provide the operational backbone your event demands.

We operate nationally, with established capabilities across NSW, QLD and VIC, and access to partner networks for international assignments.
