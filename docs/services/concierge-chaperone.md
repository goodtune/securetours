# Concierge & Chaperone

<div class="st-meta" markdown>
Concierge · Chaperone · Care
</div>

Personal, attentive care for individuals and groups who require dedicated support throughout their Australian experience.

<div class="st-service-lead" markdown>
Our Concierge & Chaperone service provides a dedicated personal support specialist for the duration of your stay. Whether navigating arrivals, coordinating business meetings, or ensuring your travel companions are always in safe hands, our chaperones are trained professionals committed to your experience.

## What's Included

- Meet and greet at airports, hotels and event venues
- Appointment, schedule and itinerary management
- Accompanied transfers — point-to-point with your dedicated chaperone
- Translation and interpreter services across a range of languages
- Medical accompaniment and health coordination
- Shopping assistance, cultural orientation and local knowledge
- Chaperoning of unaccompanied minors with approved, qualified personnel
- Group accompaniment for family visits, student tours and educational programs
- 24/7 emergency contact and support line
- Discreet service for clients who value privacy
